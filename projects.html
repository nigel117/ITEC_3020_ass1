Python

Python was my very first coding language from when I was in high school and I have loved ever since! This specific project is a card game in which the player is given 100 points and then presented with a playing card, the player must then guess whether the next card shown will have a higher or lower value than the first card, they must bet from 1 to 100 points on their guess. The game continues till either the player gambles all of their money away and therefore loses, or they succesfully gain 500 or more points within 10 rounds, if they do, they win!
